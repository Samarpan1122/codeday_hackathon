Jobs4all is a platform where you can register your job profile and need for ₹1 and buy profiles of peole for a very low cost.

Jobs4All is a user-friendly online platform enabling individuals to create ₹1 portfolios and connect with potential employers. Users can effortlessly register, build personalized portfolios showcasing their skills, experience, and accomplishments. The platform also offers an innovative feature where employers can purchase these portfolios at a nominal cost, by watching streams of the person and if like them they can buy their profile. This offers employers a cost-effective and streamlined approach to identifying suitable candidates. Job seekers benefit from increased visibility and the chance to be noticed by a broader range of employers. By facilitating a convenient exchange between job seekers and employers, Jobs4All aims to enhance the hiring process for both parties, fostering efficient connections in the job market.
